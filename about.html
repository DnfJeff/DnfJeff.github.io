Who I Am

Welcome to my corner of the internet — a space where creative exploration, digital preservation, and community-driven innovation converge.

I'm a creative technologist dedicated to transforming ideas into dynamic experiences using tools like Blender, Unreal Engine, and Python. My journey began with CSVmaker, my first software release — a lightweight utility for organizing file structures into clean CSV data.

What I Do

At OfficialComicBookNews, I built a reusable system that dynamically loads comic images and descriptions from CSV files — blending automation with creative presentation.

I'm deeply involved in archival and preservation efforts. Some of my archival and video work can be found on my YouTube channel.

My work is informed by a long-standing habit of systematic note-taking and project planning, ensuring every project is as thoughtfully curated as it is innovative.

Philosophy

This site isn't a "let's talk" pitch — it's a celebration of projects that push the boundaries of digital expression and community collaboration. It's about preserving creative outputs for the long haul and sharing the tools and knowledge that make it possible.

Thank you for exploring my world of ideas and digital craftsmanship.
